About MCAN Lagos

Empowering corps members in Lagos State through community service and professional development.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for Muslim corps members in Lagos State to develop their skills, serve their communities, and grow in their faith while contributing to national development.

Our Vision

To be the leading organization for Muslim corps members in Lagos State, fostering unity, excellence, and positive impact in society.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our actions and decisions

Faith

Upholding Islamic values and principles in all our activities.

Service

Dedicated to serving our communities and making a positive impact.

Unity

Fostering unity and collaboration among corps members.

Our History

A journey of growth and impact

Established 2025

MCAN Lagos was established to provide a platform for Muslim corps members in Lagos State to come together, support each other, and contribute to the development of their communities.
